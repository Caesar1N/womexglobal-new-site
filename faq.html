FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

WHAT IS AFRIENEXTRADFEL, AND WHAT IS ITS MISSION?

AFRIENEXTRADFEL is a premier trade fair designed to showcase African entrepreneurs and businesses. Its mission is to promote economic growth, foster innovation, and create opportunities for networking and investment across various industries in Africa.

WHEN AND WHERE WILL THE NEXT AFRIENEXTRADFEL TRADE FAIR TAKE PLACE?

The date and location for the next trade fair will be announced on our official platforms. Stay tuned for updates by visiting our website or following us on social media.

HOW CAN ENTREPRENEURS AND BUSINESSES PARTICIPATE IN THE EXHIBITION?

Businesses and entrepreneurs can register through our website or by contacting our event organizers. Registration typically involves selecting a booth package, submitting company details, and fulfilling participation requirements.

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF EXHIBITING AT AFRIENEXTRADFEL?

Exhibitors gain access to a large audience, including investors, buyers, and industry professionals. The event provides opportunities for brand exposure, business networking, direct sales, and learning from industry experts

IS THE TRADE FAIR OPEN TO INTERNATIONAL PARTICIPANTS AND INVESTORS?

Yes, AFRIENEXTRADFEL welcomes international businesses, investors, and entrepreneurs interested in exploring African markets and forming strategic partnerships.

ARE THERE SPONSORSHIP AND PARTNERSHIP OPPORTUNITIES AVAILABLE?

Absolutely. We offer sponsorship and partnership packages tailored to organizations looking to promote their brands while supporting African entrepreneurship. Contact our team for details on available opportunities.

HOW CAN I REGISTOR AS A VISITOR OR ATTENDEE?

Visitors can register online via our website or at the event venue. Early registration is encouraged to secure entry, and VIP packages may be available for enhanced access.

WILL THERE BE SEMINARS, WORKSHOPS, OR NETWORKING SESSIONS DURING THE EVENT?

Yes, AFRIENEXTRADFEL features expert-led seminars, business workshops, and networking sessions designed to equip entrepreneurs with valuable insights and connections.

WHAT INDUSTRIES AND SECTORS ARE REPRESENTED AT THE TRADE FAIR?

The exhibition covers multiple industries, including agriculture, technology, fashion, manufacturing, finance, healthcare, and more, ensuring diverse representation across Africa's economic landscape.

WHERE CAN I FIND MORE INFORMATION ABOUT AFRIENEXTRADFEL?

You can visit our official website, follow us on social media, or contact our team for inquiries regarding registration, partnerships, and event details.

Events and conferences are hubs of learning, innovation, and collaboration.

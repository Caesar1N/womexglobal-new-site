Privacy Policy

African Entrepreneurship Exposition Trade Fair Limited

The “Company” is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our attendees, exhibitors, sponsors, and website users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal data from you when you:

  • Register for our events
  • Visit our website
  • Contact us through email or phone
  • Participate in surveys or feedback forms

The personal data we collect may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Job title
  • Industry

2. Data Use

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To process event registrations and payments
  • To provide you with information about our events and services
  • To improve our website and event experiences
  • To facilitate communication with you

3. Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use industry-standard encryption technologies and secure servers.

4. Data Sharing

We may share your personal data with:

  • Event sponsors and exhibitors (with your consent)
  • Third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors)
  • Government authorities (as required by law)

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and correct your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent to processing
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing
